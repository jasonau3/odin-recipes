Pad Thai

Thai noodle stir fry with a sweet-savoury-sour sauce scattered with crushed peanuts!

  • Pad thai sauce
  • Rice noodles
  • Firm tofu
  • Garlic chives
  1. Warm up the sauce
  2. Mix the sauce with the chives and cook it in a 10 000 C° pan
  3. Mix in the tofu
  4. Pour in the pre-cooked noodles
  5. Mix and enjoy
