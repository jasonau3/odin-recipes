Yakisoba

Japanese version of stir-fried noodlesm with pork and veggies!

  • Yakisoba noodles
  • Carrot
  • Pork
  • Cabbage
  • Shittake mushrooms
  1. Warm up the sauce
  2. Mix the sauce with the pork and cook it in a 10 000 C° pan
  3. Mix in the veggies
  4. Pour in the pre-cooked noodles
  5. Mix and enjoy
